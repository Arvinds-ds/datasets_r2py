rdtelecR Documentation

rdtelec

Description

Data loads lazily. Type data(rdtelec) into the console.

Usage

data(rdtelec)

Format

A data.frame with 29 rows and 6 variables:

  • rd. R&D spending, millions $

  • sales. firm sales, millions $

  • rdintens. rd as percent of sales

  • lrd. log(rd)

  • lsales. log(sales)

  • salessq. sales^2
